From our workshops it was established that usually the admin will upload and assign an annotator one job at a time with a manageable amount of titles.

However, it is possible to assign multiple jobs to an annotator. If the tool is scaled up they may need to manage a larger number of annotators, jobs and titles.

We added a view of multiple jobs to the annotators’ ‘Your jobs’ page.

In future, we may need to find other ways to break this down if annotators have lots of jobs.
